# UX Strategy

> EcoTransit Project Docs v1.0 — generated for coding-agent handoff.
> Principle: production-grade, mobile-first, privacy-by-design, không xây trùng lõi vận hành của Go!Bus/HCMC Metro; EcoTransit là lớp trải nghiệm, nội dung, gamification và chia sẻ.


## UX vision

EcoTransit should feel like a confident, fresh, urban mobility companion for young users in TP.HCM. It should not feel like a government form, a generic SaaS dashboard, or a random map demo.

## Primary UX principles

1. Mobile-first route discovery.
2. Friendly Vietnamese microcopy.
3. Clear weather and walking burden explanation.
4. Privacy-safe sharing by default.
5. Trustworthy reward flow with visible status.
6. UGC content that feels moderated and useful.
7. Motion that clarifies transitions, not decoration overload.

## Suggested visual direction

Name: `Saigon Transit Fresh`.

- Primary: fresh teal/green-blue for movement and trust.
- Secondary: soft sky/mint backgrounds.
- Accent: warm orange/coral used sparingly for rewards or warnings.
- Neutral: off-white/off-black, no pure black/white extremes.
- Shapes: rounded but not childish; one radius system.
- Typography: strong display heading + readable body; tabular numerals for ETA/points.

## UI personality

- Energetic but not childish.
- Useful before flashy.
- Social but privacy-aware.
- Local but modern.

## Core journeys to design first

1. Route search and result comparison.
2. Station detail and guide discovery.
3. Ticket upload and verification status.
4. Points and voucher redeem.
5. Bill creation and share fallback.
6. Moderator/admin queues.
