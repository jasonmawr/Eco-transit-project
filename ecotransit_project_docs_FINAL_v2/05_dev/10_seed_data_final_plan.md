# Final Seed Data Plan

## Required seed groups

1. 14 Metro Line 1 stations.
2. Representative bus stops/routes connected to central areas.
3. Nearby POIs for key stations.
4. Guide/video sample content.
5. Review sample content in approved/pending/rejected states.
6. Voucher catalog sample.
7. Demo users: user, moderator, admin.
8. Ticket samples: valid, duplicate, low-confidence, invalid.
9. Geofence polygon config.
10. Weather scenarios for tests: normal, rain, heavy_rain, hot.

## Station seed minimum

- Bến Thành.
- Nhà hát Thành phố.
- Ba Son.
- Văn Thánh.
- Tân Cảng.
- Thảo Điền.
- An Phú.
- Rạch Chiếc.
- Phước Long.
- Bình Thái.
- Thủ Đức.
- Khu Công nghệ cao.
- Đại học Quốc gia.
- Bến xe Suối Tiên.

## Demo content required

- 3 articles.
- 3 video guide records.
- 20 POIs.
- 15 reviews across states.
- 5 vouchers.
- 10 tickets across statuses.

## Acceptance

A fresh database with seed command must make the app look alive enough for customer UAT without manual DB editing.
