# Customer Acceptance Pack

## What to show the customer

1. Mobile home and route planner.
2. Route search with bus + metro and weather explanation.
3. Station detail and nearby discovery.
4. Article/video guide tied to station/weather.
5. Ticket upload and points status.
6. Moderator approval and points ledger.
7. Voucher redemption.
8. Time bill share card.
9. Admin dashboard/moderation.

## Signoff checklist

- [ ] Customer confirms feature set matches brief.
- [ ] Customer confirms UI is modern and better than benchmark.
- [ ] Customer confirms business flows are understandable.
- [ ] Customer confirms any new desired changes are logged separately.
- [ ] Customer confirms production launch dependencies such as domain, API keys and legal text.
